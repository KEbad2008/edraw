**Whiteboard Website**

This is a simple online whiteboard you can use to draw and share ideas. You can use your mouse or finger to sketch, write, or make shapes.

The website has different tools like a pen, eraser, and shapes (line, rectangle, and circle). You can also pick from many colors or choose your own. You can change how thick your pen is and how light or dark it looks.

If you make a mistake, you can undo it or clear the whole board. When you’re done, you can download your drawing as an image.

It’s easy to use and works on both computers (click with your click and hold down while your drawing) and touch screens.
